How It Started

From a Dorm Room
to 50,000+ Channels

VidFlow didn't start in a boardroom — it started with a problem. Mohammad Zaahir, an AI engineer with a passion for data and automation, noticed that talented creators in India were being left behind by the algorithm despite putting out genuinely great content.

He brought in Hashir Siddique, an SEO strategist who understood how discoverability worked, and Anas Khan, a certified Google Ads expert who knew how to move real audiences. Together, they built something different: a growth system powered entirely by legitimate Google Ads campaigns — no bots, no shortcuts.

The early days weren't glamorous. Late nights debugging campaign logic, manual testing on real channels, reinvesting every rupee back into research. But the results spoke for themselves — channels grew, creators came back, and word spread.

Today, VidFlow serves 50,000+ creators across India. The mission has never changed: give every creator the audience they deserve.

◆
The Problem We Saw
Indian creators with incredible content were invisible. The algorithm buried them before they had a chance. We knew there had to be a better way — and we built it.
◆
The Effort We Put In
Over 5 years of testing, optimising, and refining. Hundreds of campaigns. Thousands of hours. Every rupee reinvested until we had a system that consistently delivered real, lasting results.
◆
The Standard We Set
No bots. No fake traffic. Only verified Google Ads campaigns reaching genuine, targeted audiences. We refused to cut corners — and our 98% satisfaction rate proves it was the right call.
◆
Where We Are Today
50,000+ channels grown. 2 Cr+ views delivered. A team that still treats every order like the first — because every creator's dream matters just as much as the last.
